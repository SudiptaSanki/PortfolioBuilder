Our Philosophy

At Zen Flow, we believe yoga is a journey inward. We focus on the connection between breath and movement, creating a moving meditation that heals the body and calms the mind.

Led by Maya Lin, a certified RYT-500 instructor with over a decade of experience in Vinyasa and Restorative practices. Her teachings are rooted in ancient traditions but adapted for modern life.
