About the Author

Arthur Pendelton is the author of three critically acclaimed novels. His work has been translated into over twenty languages and has won numerous awards, including the prestigious Pen/Faulkner Award for Fiction.

Born in London and raised in the Scottish Highlands, his work is heavily influenced by rugged landscapes and complex historical narratives. He currently resides in Edinburgh with his wife, two children, and an unruly golden retriever named Hemingway.

When not writing, he can be found hiking the Cairngorms or scouring dusty second-hand bookshops for forgotten classics.
