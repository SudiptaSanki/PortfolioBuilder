Our Commitment to the Earth

Leaving the planet better than we found it.

Since day one, EarthFirst has been built on the principle of radical transparency and ecological restoration. We don't just want to do "less bad" — we aim to do good.

  • Over 50,000 trees planted through our partnership with OneTreePlanted.
  • Eliminated 2 tons of single-use plastic from our supply chain.
  • 100% of our electricity comes from renewable solar and wind sources.

We are a certified B-Corporation, balancing purpose and profit every single day.
