The Beauty of Imperfection

Every vessel we create is individually thrown on the wheel or hand-pinched. We embrace the tiny variations in glaze flow, iron speckling, and form that tell the story of the clay's transition from raw earth to finished stoneware.

Our glazes are mixed from scratch using natural wood ash, silica, and field clays, resulting in deep, organic earthy finishes.
