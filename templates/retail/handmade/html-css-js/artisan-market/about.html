Our Story

Made by Hand, Made to Last

Artisan Market began in a small Portland garage in 2020. What started as a weekend hobby quickly grew into a passionate craft practice and thriving Etsy shop.

Every piece is individually crafted using traditional techniques — hand-thrown pottery, lost-wax casting, and natural dyeing processes. We believe in slow making and sustainable materials.
