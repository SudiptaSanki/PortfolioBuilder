Our Story

Three Generations of Growing

Harvest Roots began in 2018 when the Miller family converted 40 acres of conventional farmland to certified organic production. Today we grow over 60 varieties of vegetables, fruits, and herbs using regenerative agriculture practices.

We believe food should nourish both the body and the land it comes from. Our no-till methods, cover cropping, and composting build healthy soil that sequesters carbon and supports biodiversity.
