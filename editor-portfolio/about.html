The Director's Cut

My journey in film and post-production

Telling stories frame by frame

I am a freelance video editor and colorist with over 7 years of experience in the film and advertising industry. Based in Los Angeles, I specialize in bringing rhythm and emotion to raw footage.

My approach is deeply collaborative. I work closely with directors and brands to ensure the final cut not only meets the vision but elevates it. From short films to high-octane commercials, I believe every project has a unique heartbeat waiting to be found in the edit suite.

Tools: Premiere Pro, DaVinci Resolve, After Effects, Final Cut Pro
