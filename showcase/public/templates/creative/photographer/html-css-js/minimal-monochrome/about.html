The Artist

Chasing shadows and light across the globe.

Born in Milan and currently based in New York City, I've spent the last decade exploring the intersection of fashion, fine art, and human emotion through the lens of my camera.

My work is characterized by a minimalist approach, focusing on form, texture, and the subtle interplay of light. I believe that a photograph should not just capture a moment, but evoke a feeling that lingers long after.

Exhibitions

  • 2024 "Silhouettes" — MoMA PS1, New York
  • 2022 "Chiaroscuro" — Galleria Carla Sozzani, Milan
  • 2019 "Faces of Fashion" — Saatchi Gallery, London
