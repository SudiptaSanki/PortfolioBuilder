About Chef Marcus

A lifetime dedicated to the pursuit of flavor perfection.

My Journey

Trained in Michelin-starred kitchens across Tokyo and Paris, Marcus Vance brings a cross-cultural perspective to modern dining. Combining Japanese reverence for seasonal freshness with classical French technique.

Over the last decade, Marcus has run acclaimed pop-ups, worked as a private chef for discerning clients, and consulted for luxury boutique hotels.
