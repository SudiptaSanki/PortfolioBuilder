Rooted in our community.

David Sterling isn't a career politician. He's a former teacher, small business owner, and community organizer who knows what it takes to build a city that works for everyone.

Born and raised in the South Ward, David saw firsthand the impact of underfunded schools and lack of economic opportunity. After serving 10 years on the City Council, he's running for Mayor to bring transparent, accountable leadership to City Hall.

"True leadership is about listening to the most vulnerable among us, and having the courage to act."
